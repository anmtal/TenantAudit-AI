Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the automated audit? +

LeaseAlign AI extracts and compares document terms with high precision using advanced LLMs. However, AI can occasionally overlook subtle contextual details. Therefore, all comparisons are designed as assistive tools and should be verified by a human expert or legal counsel.

What is the Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) review pattern? +

Our platform displays extracted fields side-by-side with original text quotes from the lease and estoppel certificate. This allows human auditors to quickly click, verify, and override any discrepancies in a matter of seconds, rather than searching manually through 100-page lease files.

Is my transaction data secure? +

Absolutely. We employ bank-grade encryption in transit and at rest. Documents are processed entirely in memory and are never written to permanent disk storage. We never share your data or use it to train public AI models.

How does the platform guard against AI hallucinations or errors? +

We employ strict schema formatting, localized context matching, and an aggregate vision merging pipeline to isolate errors. Crucially, every extracted clause is directly linked to its exact page citation and original highlighted text quote, allowing human auditors to instantly verify accuracy in one click rather than blind trust.

Does LeaseAlign AI replace legal counsel or commercial brokers? +

No. LeaseAlign AI is an assistive productivity tool designed to automate the manual, time-consuming extraction of terms. It highlights potential discrepancies and saves up to 90% of review time, but all final audits and lease summaries should be verified by a qualified real estate professional or legal counsel.

What happens to my document privacy? Are files saved? +

We prioritize document security and data privacy. Standard PDF text extraction runs entirely client-side inside your browser. For scanned documents or advanced cross-referencing, content is transmitted securely using end-to-end encryption to our server and processed in memory. Original files are never saved on our servers, and we do not store your data or use it to train AI models.

How does the platform compare to hiring a broker or attorney? +

LeaseAlign AI acts as an assistive force multiplier for legal, brokerage, and acquisition teams. It allows you to complete initial lease-versus-estoppel reviews in seconds and flags mismatches with 99% accuracy. It is designed to save up to 90% of manual auditing time, but all final summaries should be verified by a licensed professional or legal counsel before signing.

Secure Isolated Hosting

Unlike other platforms, LeaseAlign AI extracts standard PDF text directly in your browser and processes scanned documents via secure, memory-only OCR endpoints. Your lease texts and credentials are routed via encrypted API proxy memory and are never stored on disk. Audit summary comparisons are securely saved under your own private account, significantly reducing corporate compliance and data exposure risks.
